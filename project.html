My Projects

Kojima Wars


Description: This game was created by me and our entire year group totalling around 30 people. The game consists of players fighting against other players to capture bases around the Island. These bases increase the teams total score which is used to win the game. Players are these big mechs that are able to transform into a jet version to allow the players to fly around the map. As well as PVP the players have a team cash pool that players can spend on base upgrades and AI units that can be ordered around to aid the players. Before the players spawn in they can select a class that will provide them with weapons and abilities that can be used in engagements.


My contributions to this game


- AI: Pathfinding, Island nav mesh, AI agent stats(agent speed, height, width, collision detection etc.)


- Modeling: Modeled the military buildings(infanty barracks, tank factories, and helipad) and also all the weapons that the player can use.

Rooted In Defence


Description: This game was apart of the 2023 global game jam and the game was made by me and a small team. The game is a generic tower defence game, the player has to protect their base from enemies by placing down towers that shoot at the enemies. As the theme given was that of roots, we decided to add a intresting mechanic to the game. When a tower is placed town it spawns turrets that are rooted to the built tower. because they are rooted togther if the tower is destroyed all the turrets linked to that tower will also be destroyed.


My contributions to this game


- Combat: Turret shooting, enemies attacking turrets/towers, destroying objects etc.


- Inventory/Resources: Passive resource generation, resource generation when enemies are killed, checks to see if the player has sufficent resources to build/upgrade.


- Turret Visuals/Stats: Turret types, damage, reload time, ammo type, turret visuals

The God Game


Description: This game was apart of a 2 other games that we were asked to do over my masters degree. We had to create 3 games that were at diffrent stages of developement (Alpha, Beta, Gold) this game was our gold. The player is overseeing an island of people, the island population is split up into diffrent classes. These classes provide resources that can be used by the player. During play the island is hit with diffrent natural disasters that the player will need to prepare for, the player can see on the timeline an estimate of when these disasters will take place but they will not know the severity of these disasters. at any point the player can manipulate the populations class gaining more of a particular resource, but doing so will change the populations awareness of the player. If the awarness reaches max then the player is discovered and its game over. The goal of the game is to reach the end of the timeline with the most people alive.


My contributions to this game


- Tutorial: As I was working on the Alpha and Beta primarily, I didnt have much impact on this game. I was only able to work on the tutorial that explained how to play the game.

Disc Royal


Description: This game was one of the first games me and one other course mate began development on for our potential indie studio. The game is a battle royal type game that includes mechs and melee comabat. When the game starts the player and AI is placed onto a circular platform that will shrink and fall apart over time. The player will have to hit the AI robots off the platform in order to win else fall of themseleves. The game will feature a character customisation screen that will allow the player to customise their mechs and create weapon classes. The game is PVE but the hope is to add multiplayer to reach out to a wider audiance.


My contributions to this game so far


- Combat: Directional combat similar to the Mount and Blade series or Kingdom Come Deliverance. Knockback of enemies. Baisc aniamtions for attacking and blocking


- AI: Enemy movement, enemy attacking, navmesh


- Player: Movement, Camara, free look inside the cockpit of the mech
